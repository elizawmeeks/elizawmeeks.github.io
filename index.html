About Me

I am hungry for learning.

I couldn’t do the Tolstoy-Chemistry-Fashion-Design-Biology-French major I wanted, so I settled for Art History while working as an animator and project lead in the Chemistry Department. When the chance to study Programming at Nashville Software School arose I jumped.

Technology creates an intersection for all of my interests, a central hub from which I can reach out and explore anything I care about or want to understand. It contains endless and ever-changing quantities of new things to learn.

And with it I can help people. I created Tender Tummies, an app designed to facilitate parents managing complex allergy diseases. The prospect of making apps that can help, amuse, inspire, and promote change inspires me to continue learning.

As if I’d ever stop.

Tender Tummies

A CRUD app for managing FPIES, a rare allergy disease. User inputs child’s allergy data, then the app pushes data up to firebase, then gets the data to manipulate and display it in an interconnected, concise, easy-to-read manner.
